Frequently Asked Questions

Is my cycle data private?
Yes, completely. All your data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers. We literally cannot see your information. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
Do I need to create an account?
No. Sync works entirely without an account. This is part of our privacy-first design - no accounts means no personal information stored on servers.
Does Sync work offline?
Yes! Sync works completely offline. The app never needs an internet connection to function. Your data stays on your device at all times.
How do I back up my data?
Your Sync data is included in your iPhone backup (iCloud or local). When you restore your phone from a backup, your Sync data will be restored as well.
How accurate are the predictions?
Sync uses your logged cycle data to predict your fertile window and next period. The more cycles you log, the more accurate predictions become. However, please note that Sync is not a medical device and predictions should not be used as a form of birth control.
Can I use Sync on multiple devices?
Since Sync stores data locally and doesn't use cloud sync (for privacy), each device will have its own separate data. We recommend using Sync on your primary iPhone.
Is there a subscription fee?
No. Sync is a one-time purchase of $1.99. There are no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and no ads. Pay once, use forever.
How do I delete my data?
You can delete individual entries within the app, use the reset option in Settings to clear all data, or simply delete the app from your device. Since we never have your data, deletion is immediate and permanent.
