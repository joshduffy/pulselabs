Frequently Asked Questions

Is my health data private?
Yes, completely. All your data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers. We literally cannot see your health information. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
Do I need to create an account?
No. Ember works entirely without an account. This is part of our privacy-first design - no accounts means no personal information stored on servers.
How do I back up my data?
Your Ember data is included in your iPhone backup (iCloud or local). When you restore your phone from a backup, your Ember data will be restored as well. You can also export PDF reports to save your symptom history.
Can I use Ember on multiple devices?
Since Ember stores data locally and doesn't use cloud sync (for privacy), each device will have its own separate data. We recommend using Ember on your primary iPhone.
How does Apple Health integration work?
When you enable Apple Health integration, Ember can read your sleep, heart rate, and HRV data to help identify correlations with your symptoms. This data is processed entirely on your device and never sent anywhere.
What symptoms can I track?
Ember supports 20+ perimenopause and menopause symptoms including hot flashes, night sweats, sleep disruptions, mood changes, brain fog, anxiety, joint pain, headaches, and more. You can also add custom notes to any entry.
How do I share reports with my doctor?
Go to the Reports tab, select a date range, and generate a PDF. You can then share it via email, AirDrop, print it, or save it to Files. The report is created on your device and you control how it's shared.
Is Ember free?
Yes, Ember is completely free with no in-app purchases or subscriptions. We believe every woman deserves access to quality health tracking tools.
