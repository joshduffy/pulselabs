Privacy Policy

Ember by PulseLab

The short version: Ember stores all your data locally on your device. We never collect, transmit, or have access to your personal health information. Period.

Our Privacy Philosophy

We built Ember with a simple principle: your health data is deeply personal and should never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to share it. We designed the app so that we literally cannot access your data - it's not stored on our servers because we don't have servers that store user data.

Data Stored on Your Device

Ember stores the following data locally on your iPhone:

  • Symptom entries you log (type, severity, timing, notes)
  • Any triggers or lifestyle factors you record
  • Your app preferences and settings
  • Generated insights based on your symptom patterns

This data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it (such as generating a PDF report for your healthcare provider).

Apple Health Integration

If you choose to enable Apple Health integration, Ember can read:

  • Sleep data
  • Heart rate and heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Activity and exercise data

This data is read directly from Apple Health on your device. It is never transmitted to PulseLab or any third party. Apple Health data is processed locally to provide you with insights about how your symptoms may correlate with your sleep, activity, and other health metrics.

PDF Reports

When you generate a PDF report for your healthcare provider, the report is created entirely on your device. You control how you share it - via email, AirDrop, or any other method you choose. We never see or receive these reports.

Biometric Security

Ember supports Face ID and Touch ID to protect your private health data. Biometric data is handled entirely by Apple's secure enclave and is never accessible to Ember or PulseLab.

Push Notifications

Ember offers optional push notifications to help you remember to log your symptoms. If you enable notifications:

  • Notifications are delivered through Apple Push Notification Service (APNs)
  • We send generic reminder messages only - no personal health data is included in notifications
  • Notification preferences are stored locally on your device
  • You can disable notifications at any time in the app Settings or iOS Settings

Push notification tokens are used solely for delivering reminders and are not linked to any personal information.

Third-Party Services

Ember uses Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) for optional reminder notifications. We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not contain any SDKs that collect user data.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control. To delete your data:

  • Delete individual entries within the app
  • Use the "Clear All Data" option in Settings
  • Delete the app from your device

Once deleted, data cannot be recovered because we don't have any backup of your information.

Children's Privacy

Ember is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and note the date of the last revision below. Our commitment to not collecting your data will not change.
